Mini Expansion Panel

Used to collapse small sections of content, most commonly used in a help sidebar. Mini Expansion Panels are style neutral, and are not view encapsulated, so you can override the styles to your own specification.

Single Mini Expansion Panel

Click listener toggle is handled internally. Any content can be placed inside rivet-mini-expansion-head and rivet-mini-expansion-body, as long as the parent wrapper contains those attributes (used to transclude content).

setting panelOpen to true will open the expansion panel by default
